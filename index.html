Welcome to What Should I Watch?

Tired of spending 30 minutes deciding what to watch? Add your backlog, hit the button, and let fate decide for you.

💾
Your data lives in this browser Your movie and TV series lists are saved in your browser's local storage — nothing is sent to any server.
⚠️
Clearing your browser data will wipe your list If you clear site data, cookies, or local storage — your list will be gone. There's no cloud backup, so add carefully!
🖥️
Each browser has its own list Opening this on a different browser or device will start with a fresh default list — your changes don't sync across browsers.
📚
Pre-loaded samples included Get started with curated sample movies and TV series. You can add your own titles or remove the defaults anytime via the library.
